*2 columns
-Research papers
-Books

Syntax: \documentclass[twocolumn]{...}

Example:
\documentclass[twocolumn]{article}
\usepackage{lipsum}
\begin{document}
	\lipsum[1-14]
\end{document}

*Multi Column
-Magazine
-Newspapers

Syntax: 
\begin{multicols{<No.ofColumns>}
...
\end{multicols}

Example: 
%\documentclass[twocolumn]{article}
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{lipsum}
\usepackage{multicol}
\begin{document}
	\begin{multicols}{3}
	\lipsum[1-14]
	\end{multicols}
\end{document}

*Adding space between the columns

%\documentclass[twocolumn]{article}
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{lipsum}
\usepackage{multicol}
\begin{document}
	\setlength{\columnsep}{35pt}
	\begin{multicols}{3}
	\lipsum[1-14]
	\end{multicols}
\end{document}


%\documentclass[twocolumn]{article}
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{lipsum}
\usepackage{multicol}
\begin{document}
	\setlength{\columnsep}{20pt}
	\setlength{\columnseprule}{1pt}
	\begin{multicols}{3}
	\lipsum[1-14]
	\end{multicols}
\end{document}
